Pentaguards Trading-Card Game

Looking for a new and exciting trading card game that combines the best of AI-generated art and interactive gameplay? Look no further than Pentaguards TCG! Playable through a Discord Bot, this community-run game allows players to collect stunning AI artworks from a wide range of artists, categories, and collections. And with an AI-generated world synopsis based on your deck and dynamically generated card and frame combinations, the possibilities are limitless. But the fun doesn't stop there - Pentaguards TCG also offers a variety of community features like trading, guilds, treasure chests, airdrops, and custom cards to keep you engaged and connected with other players. And the best part? It's completely free to play, with optional donations to support the game and its development. So why wait? Join the action today!
